# Sanjeev AI - Product Requirements Document ## Project Overview Sanjeev AI is a revolutionary platform that monitors both physical and mental health through medication tracking. It solves the problem of dangerous drug interactions and "prescription cascades" where new medicines are prescribed to treat side effects of old ones without the connection being made. ## Target Audience - Patients visiting multiple doctors. - Caregivers monitoring family medication. - Individuals concerned about the mental health side effects of physical medications. ## Design Philosophy: "Modern Ayurveda" - **Trustworthy:** Professional layout and clear data visualization. - **Calm & Healing:** Earthy tones (Deep Green, Sandalwood Beige) and soft UI elements. - **Intelligent but Simple:** AI features are powerful but explained in plain language. - **Cultural Resonance:** Subtle Ayurvedic motifs (lotus, leaves) and a premium, traditional-meets-modern feel. ## Core Features & Screens ### 1. Home Dashboard - **Greeting:** Personalized welcome. - **Medication Risk Score:** A visual meter (Green/Yellow/Red) showing the overall safety of the current medication mix. - **Quick Actions:** Floating or prominent buttons for "Scan Prescription", "Check Mood", and "View Alerts". - **Current Meds Summary:** A clean list of what to take next. ### 2. Prescription Scanner - **Camera Interface:** Soft, guided overlay for capturing photos of prescriptions. - **OCR Integration:** Visual feedback showing the AI "reading" the text. - **Upload Option:** Ability to pick from the gallery. ### 3. Risk Analysis Screen - **Status Indicator:** High-visibility result (Safe / Caution / Dangerous). - **The "Why":** Plain language explanation of interactions (e.g., "These two drugs together may affect your heart rhythm"). - **Prescription Cascade Warning:** Specific flag if a drug appears to be treating a side effect of another. ### 4. Mood Tracker - **Input:** Simple, 30-second check-in using emojis or a slider. - **Visualization:** A soft, calming weekly mood graph. - **Voice/Text Log:** Option to record thoughts for deeper AI analysis. ### 5. Timeline View (History) - **Unified View:** A vertical timeline merging medication starts/changes with mood fluctuations. - **Correlation Bridge:** Highlights like "Your mood dip started 10 days after starting Drug X." ### 6. Alert Screen - **Urgent Warnings:** Clear but non-aggressive UI for dangerous interactions. - **Action Items:** One-tap buttons to "Notify Doctor", "Connect to Helpline", or "Alert Caregiver". - **PDF Generation:** Option to create a report for the next doctor's visit. ## Visual Assets - Earthy textures (parchment, soft paper). - Organic shapes and rounded corners. - Minimalist botanical icons.
